Quizlet 2.0

As a part of the Educational Technology course, my team and I redesigned Quizlet's learn feature to incorporate better feedback giving. The current Quizlet's learn feature does not include good formative or summative feedback, so we decided to integrate Quizlet's AI feature with the traditional flashcard style of Quizlet along with some other changes.
