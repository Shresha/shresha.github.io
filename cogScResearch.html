Emotions and Decision Making


Dive into my research project examining the nuanced relationship between emotions and decision-making. The study explores the influence of emotions on cognitive processes, actions, and the choices we make. By addressing fundamental questions, such as the impact of emotions on decision-making and the unique effects of different emotional states, the project delves into psychological, computational, and neurological dimensions. Rooted in evidence, it aims to contribute valuable insights to academic discourse, providing a comprehensive understanding of how emotions shape our decision-making processes.
