Analyzing & predicting the popularity of Songs on Spotify


This paper delves into the realm of song popularity, a crucial metric for both artists and recording labels seeking wider recognition. By employing statistical metrics and machine learning models, my team and I aimed to unravel the factors influencing a song's popularity on Spotify. Linear Regression, Random Forest Regression, and Neural Networks served as our predictive tools, with statistical methods like Mean Squared Error, Mean Absolute Error, and R-Squared gauging model accuracy.
